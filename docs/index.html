About me

I’m currently a PhD student working at the LuCE research group at USI (Lugano, Switzerland) advised by professor Matthias Hauswirth. At LuCE we do research at the boundary of programming languages and computer science education.

My research focuses on the use of techniques from Programming Language Theory to construct sound Notional Machines.

I have also done a whole lot of other things in the past, most of which are superficially described in my LinkedIn profile.
